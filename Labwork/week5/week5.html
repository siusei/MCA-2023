Week 5 - Standards in Curation

Creating a Metadata Schema for my MEI file

Considering my group of audience (may be: Studio Ghibli fans, musician, classical music and movie lover), I decided to include the following metadata elements in my MEI file:

Title: The name of my chosen piece.
Composer: The name of the person who composed this piece.
Encoder: The name of the person who encoded this file.
Date: The date this piece was published.
Location: The geographical location
Perform medium: The musical instrument this music play upon.
Genre: Music category of the piece.
Tempo: The speed of this music.
Key: The major or minor scale of this music piece.


The selected elements are to let my audiences to understand the basic music property and published background of this music.
